We're in this together

I took Rodney’s and smiled as we walked forward to collect it.

“This feels right,” he said. “I’m so glad I have someone to share this moment with me. I know we used to be enemies, but you’re my best friend.”

“I’d be flattered if it wasn’t so embarrassing,” I laughed. I nudged his shoulder, pushing down everything I felt on the inside. He laughed with me.

“I know I didn’t think much of you yesterday, but you truly are an incredible spell caster and thanks to you, we’ll be able to make the world a better place.”

“We?”

“If you do me the honor of being my partner,” he said, “I’d love to go on many more adventures with you.”

You smiled, all the jealousy and negativity collecting and dissipating inside of you. “Oh, Rodney, I’d love to go on more adventures with you.”

And together, you grabbed the jewel, laughing as its power surged inside of both of you. It was exhilarating.

“Together,” he promised, “we’ll make the world better.”

“Together,” you agreed.

The End.
